About Me

I am currently pursuing an MS by Research in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, specializing in the Intelligent Systems stream under the guidance of Prof. Virendra Singh.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with a minor in Data Science and Engineering from IISER Bhopal.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Large Language Models. My current work focuses on understanding and improving the interpretability of LLMs, making these models more transparent, reliable, and trustworthy.

I enjoy solving challenging research problems using analytical thinking, collaborative teamwork, and practical implementation. I primarily work with Python and C++ for AI research and software development.
